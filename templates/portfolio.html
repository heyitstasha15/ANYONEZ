PRETTYMUCH

PRETTYMUCH is an American-Canadian pop boy band based in L.A, California. They have been living together for the past two years, making music and having fun all together. They started out as solo artists in their home areas until they each caught Simon Cowell's eye. They all are dancers and do covers of famous songs.

The group consist of

  1. ZION Kuwonu
  2. BRANDON Arreaga
  3. AUSTIN Porter
  4. EDWIN Honoret &
  5. NICK Mara

The band was formed by Simon Cowell in 2016,just two years after the break of world-famous boy band One Direction. The boys of PRETTYMUCH are from all over in the U.S with one bandmate being from Ottawa, Canada. Their ages range from 18-20
